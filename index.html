A Full Stack Solution

An indigenous drive train solution for e-mobility, which is efficient, flexible, modular, and scalable.

Innovation at the Core

We provide drive train solutions for motors ranging from 1KW to 15KW. A vehicle control unit or drive train system for agile performance/driving experience of vehicles, which encompasses cross-functional coordination with all driving elements of two-wheeler such as BMS, Motor, thermal management, torque control, charging management, Speed Control, Range Control, and Dashboard (Smart Predictive strategy). And these all comes along with cloud-based solution with mobile app integration.

BLDC Hub Motor

  • A scalable Hub Motor which can apply from Scooters to Mopeds
  • Max rated power up to 13KW
  • Best-in-class torque of 172 Nm - 220 Nm
  • Enables speeds up to12 Km/hr
  • Can be configured to 48V, 60V, and 72V

Modular Battery

  • Safest LMFP Battery Chemistry! Resistant to Weather, Water, and Fire.
  • AIS 156 Phase II Certified
  • 2C Discharge Capable
  • Smart BMS with Active Cell Balancing
  • Can be configurable to 48V, 60V, and 72V
  • Auto cutoff with audio buzzer
  • CAN Enabled

Controller

  • CAN bus/RS485 controller area network communication function.
  • GOTAC transmitting real-time vehicle status to mobile app via cloud.
  • OBD (On-board diagnostics) communication function.
  • Voice command unlock enabled using GPS module.
  • GPS module transmitting all vehicle data to cloud.
